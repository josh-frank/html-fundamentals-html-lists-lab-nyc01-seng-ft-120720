Sauerkraut grilled cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • 3 oz. sauerkraut
  • 2 tbsp. butter
  1. Heat 1 tbsp. butter in pan; fry bread for 1 minute
  2. Layer cheese slices and bread, with sauerkraut in the middle
  3. Fry until golden brown, then serve with melted butter for dipping
